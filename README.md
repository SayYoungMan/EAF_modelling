# Electric Arc Furnace (EAF) Modelling
 
## Introduction

Modelling of Electric Arc Furnace (EAF) in MATLAB based on the following papers:

* Logar, Dovzan and Skrjanc, *Modeling and Validation of an Electric Arc Furnace: Part 1, Heat and Mass Transfer*, March 2012, ISIJ International 52(3):402-412.

    * URL: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/233391599_Modeling_and_Validation_of_an_Electric_Arc_Furnace_Part_1_Heat_and_Mass_Transfer

* Logar, Dovzan and Skrjanc, *Modeling and Validation of an Electric Arc Furnace: Part 2, Thermo-chemistry*, March 2012, ISIJ International 52(3):413-423.

    * URL: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/233391600_Modeling_and_Validation_of_an_Electric_Arc_Furnace_Part_2_Thermo-chemistry

Although some aspects of the papers are either simplified or neglected to limit the scope to be covered in the Final Year Design Project, the model provides satisfactory accuracy.
